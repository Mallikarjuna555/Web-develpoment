ITC Grand Hotels:defines Luxury

ITC's Hotel Group is the hospitality business of ITC Limited. With the Namaste as the enduring symbol of its brand experience, ITC's Hotel Group is one of India’s leading luxury hotel chains. It operates under four distinct brands with more than 100 hotels in over 70 destinations in the country. Integrating India’s fine tradition of hospitality with globally benchmarked services, the luxury hotels reflect the culture and ethos of each destination.
